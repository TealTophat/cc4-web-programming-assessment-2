The End of Stay Puft? Ray Stantz on the Matter...



After the devastating attack on New York city, Gozer the Gozerian has finally fallen. But people are all asking the same question: Why Stay Puft?

For those unaware, Gozer the Gozerian was one of the most powerful ghosts the Ghostbusters have ever fought. In an attempt to destroy New York City, she had taken the form of the beloved Stay Puft man from the name brand marshmallows. In this article, we will dive into the facts and determine the fate of the entire Stay Puft franchise, with commentary and advice from ghost expert Ray Stantz.
