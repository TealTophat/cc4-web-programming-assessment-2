EXCLUSIVE! An Interview with Egon Spengler on the technology of the Ghostbusters



Dr. Egon Spengler is widely known as the brains of the Ghostbusters team.
But the question is: How has his innovative technology contributed to the success of the Ghostbusters?

There are many pieces of technology that Dr. Spengler has created for the Ghostbusters. Some examples are the P.K.E. meter that allows them to see the ghosts they pursue, as well as the photon rays and traps they use to combat and contain those ghosts. This article will dive into the creation of this technology, and how Egon came to make them.
